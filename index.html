Fandom through the lens of Fanfiction

In 1998 the website FanFiction.Net began. While other fanfiction hosting sites already existed at the time, this was one of the first effective attempts to store fanfiction on the web for many different fandoms in the same place. Since then millions of fics have been shared.

Through the power of the Wayback Machine internet archive, we can track the flow of fics posted to FanFiction.Net. For fanfiction to be produced for a fandom, the fandom must have highly invested fans. Thus the measure of the flow of fanfiction being shared can give us insights into specific fandoms.
