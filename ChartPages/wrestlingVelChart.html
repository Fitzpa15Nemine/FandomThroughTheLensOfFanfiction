Wrestling Fic Count Changes over time


The Variable Release Fandom

Wrestling remains the most popular "misc" fandom on FanFiction.Net, a fandom with a history back near the start of the website. Punctuated with colorful story lines, wrestling is also the only sport to have an archive on FanFiction.Net as the stories are about fictional characters.

While early FanFiction.Net did have categories for Music Groups and other "real life" people they eventually adopted a policy that bars fanfiction about real people from their site.

The negative spikes do illustrate that it is possible to remove fanfiction from the site and have negative growth in a fandom, although usually the amount removed doesn't exceed what is added. It is also possible some of the bounces observed here are do to category shifts or other data anomolies which would require more investigation.
