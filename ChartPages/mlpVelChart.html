My Little Pony Fic Count Changes over time


The Sudden Release Fandom

My Little Pony is interesting as the fandom as a whole has existed since toys were first introduced in 1981. However, while people were certainly writing there own stories for the toys, the lack of a centralized story meant there was not much written that was specifically "fanfiction." That is until "My Little Pony: Friend is Magic" (itself ironically an original fic inspired by the toys) was created.

The explosion of popularity in this fandom with the release of MLP: FiM very clear in the fanfiction count records, and the fandom still maintains some following to this day. Notably, this is also a case where there are a lot of popular fandom specific locations for posting fan derivative works that are very active, outside of FanFiction.Net.
